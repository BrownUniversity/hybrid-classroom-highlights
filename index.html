Hybrid classroom

Explore the hybrid classroom

Click on the dots to learn more about aspects of teaching remote and in-person students simultaneously.

Connect to room audio and video

Connect your laptop to the room equipment and start your Zoom session.

Use tablet for writing and drawing

If you need to write notes for students, use a Wacom tablet. Remote students will not be able to adequately see writing on a blackboard or whiteboard.

Remote students visiable via large display

Your remote students will be viewable on the room projector to the side of your presentation.

In-person students joining Zoom

Physically present students should join the Zoom meeting, but should mute their microphones.

Using installed cameras

The rear mounted camera will give remote students a view of the classroom, but is insufficient for viewing any wall writing.

Teaching assistants

TAs maybe able to help manage questions and comments from remote students.

Social distancing

All people in the classroom should maintain adequate social distancing according to University guidelines.
